Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties do you handle in your real estate services?
We specialize in a wide range of real estate properties including residential homes, commercial buildings, plots of land, and rental apartments. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, lease, or invest, our experienced agents are here to guide you every step of the way.
How can I get an insurance policy through your company?
Getting insured with us is easy. Simply reach out through our website or visit our office for a free consultation. We will assess your needs and recommend the best coverage plan for your property, business, vehicle, or life, ensuring maximum protection at competitive rates.
Do you assist with property leasing and tenant management?
Yes, we offer complete leasing solutions including tenant placement, background checks, rent collection, and property maintenance support. Our goal is to help property owners enjoy hassle-free income from their investments.
What does your business insurance cover?
Our business insurance policies cover risks such as fire, theft, liability, equipment damage, and employee injury. We tailor each policy to fit your business needs, ensuring you're protected from unexpected disruptions.
Can I combine real estate and insurance services into a single package?
Absolutely. We offer bundled service packages that combine property acquisition or leasing with relevant insurance coverage. This helps you save time and money while ensuring every aspect of your property or business is fully protected.
